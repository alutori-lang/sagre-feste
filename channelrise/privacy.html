Privacy Policy

App: ChannelRise
Last updated: May 31, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the ChannelRise app ("the App") handles your data. We respect your privacy and process data in line with the GDPR (Regulation (EU) 2016/679) and Apple's App Store requirements.

1. Data controller

The data controller is Adnan Riaz. For any privacy request, contact alutori@gmail.com.

2. Data we collect

  • Account data: when you sign in, we collect an email address and a user identifier through our authentication provider (Firebase Authentication).
  • App activity: your coin balance, completed tasks, and the YouTube channels/videos you choose to promote, stored to operate the community and prevent fraud.
  • YouTube public data: when you submit a channel or video, we read publicly available statistics (e.g. subscriber and view counts) via the official YouTube Data API to verify tasks. We do not access your private YouTube data or post on your behalf.
  • Advertising & usage identifiers: Google AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to show ads and measure performance.

3. Purposes

  • To provide the App's features (earning and spending coins, promoting content).
  • To verify engagement tasks and prevent cheating/fraud.
  • To process in-app purchases via Apple.
  • To show ads (for free users) and measure their performance.

4. Third-party services

We use the following providers, each with its own privacy policy:

  • Google Firebase (authentication, database) — firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Google AdMob (advertising) — policies.google.com/privacy
  • YouTube Data API (public statistics) — subject to Google's privacy policy.

5. Data retention

We keep account and activity data for as long as your account exists. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

6. Your rights

Under the GDPR you may access, correct, or delete your data, and object to or restrict processing. To exercise these rights, email alutori@gmail.com.

7. Children

The App is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your country). We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Relationship with YouTube

ChannelRise is independent and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by YouTube or Google LLC.

9. Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be reflected on this page with a new "last updated" date.
